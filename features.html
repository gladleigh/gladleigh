Home Comforts 5 Spacious Bedrooms

Each of the bedrooms have at least a double bed or bigger and one of them has 2 single beds. The 2 bedrooms in the flat area have access to a kitchen as well as a bathroom with double basin, toilet and shower. The main bedroom inside the house has a private balcony and access to a full en-suite bathroom. The remaining 2 rooms inside the house have direct access to a bathroom with basin, toilet and shower.

Delicious Home Cooked Food Full Kitchen

The kitchen inside the main house is a large open space with sufficient appliances, cookery and crockery for you to make use of while whipping up a deliscious home cooked meal. There is also access to a Dishwasher, Washing Maching and Drier.

For the outside entertainment Large Deck or Covered Patio

The house has a large open deck looking out over Wilderness Village and the Indian Ocean and boasts some of the most amazing sunsets you will see. For days when you would prefer some shelter or a Braai (South African Barbeque) ther is also a semi enclosed balcony on othe other side of the house.
